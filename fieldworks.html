Field Work Experience

Selected Field Veterinarian

Mission Rabies Bangladesh – Mass Dog Vaccination Campaign
Kutupalong Registered Refugee Camp, Cox’s Bazar | May 26–29, 2025

  • Selected as one of the official veterinarians for a high-priority international campaign led by the Worldwide Veterinary Service (WVS) in collaboration with the DGHS Bangladesh and Obhoyaronno.
  • Contributed to the goal of vaccinating 20,000+ dogs in 4 days to break the rabies transmission cycle in a densely populated refugee area.
  • Participated in field-level vaccination, data recording using the Mission Rabies App, GPS tracking, and geofenced deployment strategies.
  • Gained practical exposure in large-scale animal health operations, real-time data-driven disease control, and One Health collaboration in a challenging field setting.

Volunteer Veterinarian – Community Goat Vaccination Camp

Community Goat Vaccination Camp
Boyra Bazar, Mymensingh | Leo Club of Bangladesh Agricultural University | 19 January 2024

  • Participated in a free vaccination and veterinary medical camp organized by the Leo Club of BAU.
  • Vaccinated 300+ goats against Peste des Petits Ruminants (PPR) to promote herd immunity in a rural community.
  • Engaged with local farmers, raising awareness about disease prevention and livestock care.
  • Contributed to grassroots animal health services and hands-on disease control in small ruminants

Field Internship Experience

Intern Veterinary Doctor – Government Appointed

Eid-ul-Azha Cattle Market (গরুর হাট), Dhaka
Ministry of Fisheries and Livestock, Government of Bangladesh | June 2024

  • Officially selected and appointed by the Ministry of Livestock to serve as a veterinary intern in one of Dhaka’s largest Eid-ul-Azha cattle markets, during peak animal trade.
  • Performed health screenings and ante-mortem inspections of livestock to ensure animal health and prevent zoonotic disease transmission.
  • Assisted in diagnosing conditions such as Foot-and-Mouth Disease (FMD), Hemorrhagic Septicemia (HS), and Black Quarter (BQ).
  • Ensured animal welfare compliance, provided public awareness on safe animal handling, and worked with multidisciplinary teams under a high-pressure, real-time setting.
  • Contributed to national efforts ensuring safe food supply and public health protection during one of the largest livestock movement events in Bangladesh.
